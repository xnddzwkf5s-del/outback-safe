# Settlement — Not Applicable to Outback Travel

> **🇦🇺 Australia Note:** This page was from the original post-collapse survival framework and is not applicable to Australian outback travel.

## Why This Page Is Not Relevant

The original "Settlement" section describes survival strategies for groups of 500–1 000 people — agriculture, formal government, permanent infrastructure, and industrial-scale resource management. This is a **post-apocalyptic model** and has no relevance to Australian outback travel.

### What Applies Instead

If you are organising a large outback convoy (10–15+ vehicles), see instead:

- **[Small Group](01-immediate/07-group-management/small-group)** — 2–5 vehicle convoys (standard Australian outback travel)
- **[Large Group](01-immediate/07-group-management/large-group)** — Tag-along tours and club trips (10–15+ vehicles)
